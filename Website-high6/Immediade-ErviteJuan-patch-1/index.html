About the Website

This website is all about the first response for the people diagnosed with autism.

What is Autism?

Autism is a developmental disorder characterized by difficulties with social interaction and communication, and by restricted and repetitive behavior.

Ang Autism ay isang developmental disorder na nailalarawan sa mga paghihirap sa pakikipag-ugnay sa lipunan at komunikasyon, at ng pinaghihigpitan at paulit-ulit na pag-uugali.

What are the signs?

The core symptoms of autism are:
- Social communication challenges
- Restricted, repetitive behaviors.

Ang pangunahing sintomas ng autism ay:
- Mga hamon sa komunikasyon sa lipunan
- Pinaghihigpitan, paulit-ulit na pag-uugali.
